📋 MCA CET — Official Exam Rules
01Exam Mode: Computer-Based Test (CBT) conducted at designated exam centres.
02Questions: 100 MCQs with 4 options each (A, B, C, D).
03Marking: +2 for correct answer. Zero negative marking.
04Total Marks: 200 (100 × 2).
05Duration: 90 minutes. Timer starts when exam begins.
06Subjects: Mathematics (25Q) • Computer Science (25Q) • Logical Reasoning (25Q) • English (25Q).
07Navigation: You can move freely between all questions during the exam.
08Answer Change: You can change your answer any number of times before submission.
09Unattempted: Blank questions get 0 marks — no penalty.
10Eligibility: BCA / B.Sc. (CS/IT/Maths) with min. 45% marks (40% for reserved).
11Admit Card: Carry valid Admit Card + government photo ID to exam centre.
12Devices: Mobiles, calculators, smartwatches strictly NOT allowed.
13Reporting: Arrive at least 30 minutes before exam time. Late entry may be denied.
14Rough Work: Rough sheets provided at centre. No personal stationery allowed.
15Result: Merit list based on marks. Final admission via counselling and seat availability.
